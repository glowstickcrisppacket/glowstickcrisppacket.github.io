Ingredients:

Gerred's magic wall of picture frames. Daz and Adam's face replacement software. Short-throw projector and Resolume VJ software.

Process:

An interactive wall of picture frames replaces the face of anyone who approaches with a random animal face. Created for and installed at Rumpus vol 23.

Custom software built using openFrameworks uses ofxCv for facetracking to add animal masks to faces, and optical flow to distort masks based on detected face movements.
