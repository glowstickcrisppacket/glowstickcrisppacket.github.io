Ingredients:

custom LED fixture, kaleidosphere

Process:

The Kaleidosphere is an impressive, advanced Kaleidoscope that is a specific tapered mirror design that creates a sphere inside. When combined with custom LED lighting it creates many magical designs, and programmed pattens cause the effect to move and evolve.
